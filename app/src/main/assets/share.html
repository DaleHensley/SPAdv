Share

Who: The primary translator or phone owner along with anybody else interested in distributing the videos.

 

Purpose: The purpose of the Share phase is to view and distribute the videos to others in your language community.

 

Activities:

  1. 1.Tap the triangle play button on the line with a video file name to view that video. 

  2. 2.View a video all the way through before you share it to make sure that it looks and sounds the way it is supposed to.  

  3. 3.If a video has mistakes in it, delete that video.  Make revisions in the dramatize and finalize phases, and create a new video. 

  4. 4.Tap the arrow button to share it.  You will have several offline and online options for sharing: 

    1. 1.Connecting your phone to another phone via bluetooth is the safest way to share offline 

    2. 2.If you have an email account and ability to connect to the internet, you can share by uploading your video to Youtube or FB or attaching it to an email. 

  5. 5.Another way to share your video is through swapping SD cards
